Ingredients

16 oz linguini cooked
1/2 cup butter softened
4 chicken breasts cooked and diced
2 cans cream of chicken soup
2 cups sour cream
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1/2 cup chicken broth
2 tbsp Parmesan cheese grated
2 cups Mozzarella cheese shredded

Instructions

Step 1
Cook noodles according to box instructions. Set aside.

Step 2
In a medium bowl, combine Â½ cup butter, cooked chicken, soup, sour cream, salt, pepper and chicken broth. Mix well and then add in cooked noodles.

Step 3
Spray a 9x13 pan and pour in noodle mixture. Sprinkle cheeses on top. Bake at 350 for 40-45 minutes.
